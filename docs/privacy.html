Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Data Collection

PaperCoat does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. All processing is performed locally on your machine. No data ever leaves your device.

PDF Processing

When you run PaperCoat on a PDF file, the text is extracted and processed entirely on your local machine. No content from your PDFs is ever sent to external servers.

Telemetry

PaperCoat contains no telemetry, analytics, or usage tracking of any kind. There are no phone-home mechanisms, no crash reporters, and no anonymous usage statistics.

Third-Party Access

PaperCoat does not use any third-party services, APIs, or external resources during operation. The only network requests are those you explicitly trigger (e.g., installing via curl or cargo).

Updates

This privacy policy may be updated occasionally. The latest version will always be published at this URL.
